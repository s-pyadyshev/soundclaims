Local presence

At Soundclaims, we believe that true success in debt recovery comes from being present where it matters most. With a network spanning over 40 countries, we bring expert local knowledge, legal expertise, and hands-on service directly to the heart of Africa’s business hubs.
Where we
operate

Soundclaims has a robust presence across Africa, including:

  • North Africa: Algeria, Egypt, Morocco, Tunisia
  • West Africa: Benin, Burkina Faso, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Nigeria, Senegal, Togo
  • Central Africa: Cameroon, Central African Republic, Chad, Congo, Gabon
  • East Africa: Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, Ethiopia
  • Southern Africa: South Africa, Angola, Namibia, Zimbabwe, Zambia

Local teams

Our boots on
the ground

Our local teams operate with a ‘boots on the ground’ mindset, ensuring that every case receives dedicated, in-region attention. Our specialists understand local laws, business practices, and cultural dynamics. This enables fast, effective, and transparent claims resolution tailored to each market.
